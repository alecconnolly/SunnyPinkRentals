About Our Flower Bouquet Cart

Create a memorable experience with our charming DIY Flower Bouquet Cart! Perfect for bridal showers, baby showers, birthdays, graduation parties, weddings, retirement celebrations, corporate events, and more.

Guests can build their own custom bouquets by choosing from a beautiful assortment of fresh flowers displayed in individual buckets on our cart. It's a fun, interactive activity that doubles as stunning event décor and a thoughtful take-home favor.

Features:

  • White flower bouquet cart
  • Flower display buckets/vases
  • Interactive DIY experience for guests
  • Perfect photo opportunity and event décor
  • Thoughtful take-home favors for your guests

Perfect for: Bridal showers, baby showers, birthdays, graduation parties, weddings, retirement celebrations, corporate events, and any special occasion!
