About Our Triple Waterslide

Get triple the fun with our premium triple waterslide! This impressive inflatable water attraction features three unique slides, each offering a different water experience—sprayers, dump buckets, and water walls. Perfect for large events and guaranteed to keep everyone entertained!

Features:

  • Triple climbing wall with padded handles and sturdy footholds
  • Three unique slides: water sprayer, dump bucket, and water wall
  • Extra-large splash pool base
  • Durable, double-stitched material for long-lasting use
  • Heavy-duty blower inflates in less than 2 minutes
  • Includes storage bag for easy transport
  • Specifications: 22'5" long x 12'8" wide x 8'9" high

Perfect for: Community festivals, large birthday parties, corporate events, school picnics, neighborhood celebrations, and any gathering where maximum fun is needed! Kids aged 4-12 will have a blast!

💧 Versatile Use: Can be used wet with a water source for splash-filled fun, or dry for bouncing and climbing adventures!

⚠️ Important: Requires adequate outdoor space and level ground for setup. CANNOT be set up on cement, gravel, or hard surfaces. Damages to the inflatables will result in fees. Please ensure your venue meets these requirements when booking.
